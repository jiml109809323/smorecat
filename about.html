Our story

Made to feel thoughtful and fun

S'moreCat is a small handmade jewellery brand created for sweet little gifts between friends.

Handmade with care

Every piece starts with a simple idea: make jewellery that feels bright, giftable, and special without losing that homemade charm.

Made for giving

The collection is designed for kids aged 5 to 13 who want something fun and lovely to share with a friend.

Growing gently

We are starting with rings and plan to add bracelets later, keeping the brand easy to grow one joyful piece at a time.
